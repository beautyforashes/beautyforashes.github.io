Introduction

"beauty for ashes" is a three-fireside study of the doctrine and history of atonement theology in the Christian church, particularly the LDS. This page contains the readings and commentary from the lectures as they proceed.

Our primary goal will be to explore the models which men have created to try and understand the Atonement of Jesus Christ: both what he did for us and how his salvation works.

Agenda

    • Ransom/Transactional
    • Hebrew
    • Mediæval
    • Satisfaction
    • Penal Substitution
    • Expiative
    • Arminian
    • Restoration
